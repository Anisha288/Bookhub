About Project

Book reading has transformed from Paper editions to electronic formats and contemporary reading is not just limited to Personal Computers but to various handheld devices like Tablets and Mobiles. Bookhub was established in 2020 with the vision to provide an extensive library of books in digital format for free on the Internet. Its been a short time we have come up with this digital library, we are still going strong in our mission to provide a valuable service to our readers. Our mission is to transform the most popular works of legendary authors to modern reading room. We publish pdf books on many subjects for readers of all ages including Fiction,mythology,programming languages and many more and offcourse we do have a section for your kids. We want that the youngest generation should also have the habit of reading story books. We have designed the website with easy to navigate interface and our responsive web design let you access the website with devices of any screen size including Laptops, Tablets and Mobiles.
